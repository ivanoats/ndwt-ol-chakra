# Leave No Trace

Boating is popular and growing. Adopting the **Leave No Trace** principles
reduces the environmental impact of paddling and camping along the
Northwest Discovery Water Trail and keeps the corridor enjoyable for
everyone who follows.

## The seven principles

1. **Plan ahead and prepare.**
2. **Travel and camp on durable surfaces.**
3. **Dispose of waste properly.**
4. **Leave what you find.**
5. **Minimize campfire impacts.**
6. **Respect wildlife.**
7. **Be considerate of other visitors.**

The full text of each principle is available from the
[Leave No Trace Center for Outdoor Ethics](https://lnt.org/why/7-principles/).
